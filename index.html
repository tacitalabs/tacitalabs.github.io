Featured product

URLStrip

URLStrip removes tracking junk from links before you open them, save them, or send them to somebody else.

What it does

URLStrip checks links for known tracking parameters and redirect patterns, removes what should not be there, and keeps the parts a URL actually needs to function.

  • Removes common trackers like utm_* and similar parameters
  • Unwraps many redirect and tracking links
  • Preserves functional state and content parameters when needed
  • Lets users report missed trackers from inside the app

How it works

On desktop, URLStrip is built to stay out of the way. Copy a link, share a link, or paste a link into the app and it cleans the tracking junk locally on the device. On iPhone, the app adds quick cleaning, QR intake, rules control, onboarding, and visibility into what was removed.

  • macOS for quiet day-to-day cleanup
  • Windows with the same local cleaning model
  • iPhone with stats, controls, and guided setup

How to use it

  1. Install the app for your platform.
  2. Copy or share a link as you normally would.
  3. Let URLStrip clean the tracking junk locally.
  4. Open, save, or share the cleaner result.

Some parameters are intentionally kept because they control content, restore state, or are required for the destination to function.

Rules and coverage

URLStrip is grounded in the upstream ClearURLs ruleset and then extended with additional maintained coverage where it improves practical results.

That gives the app a strong public baseline while still leaving room for practical tuning, additional rules, and better real-world results.

FAQ

Short answers to the obvious questions

Does URLStrip send my links anywhere?

No. The point is to clean links locally instead of routing your browsing data through another service.

Do I need an account?

No. Download it, install it, and use it. URLStrip is free, and privacy tools do not need to become a subscription relationship by default.

Where do the rules come from?

URLStrip builds on the upstream ClearURLs ruleset and extends it with additional maintained coverage where that improves practical results.

Why make another privacy tool?

Because too many privacy products solve one problem by creating another. Tacita Labs is focused on tools that reduce exposure without demanding more of your data.
