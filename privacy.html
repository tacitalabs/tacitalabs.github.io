Privacy

What we collect. What we do not.

This is the plain-language version. Short on purpose. Clear on purpose.

URLStrip, the app

  • Links cleaned by URLStrip do not need to leave your device.
  • The app is designed to work locally instead of routing links through a cloud service.
  • There is no account required to use URLStrip.
  • The product is meant to reduce exposure, not create a new dependency.

This website

  • The site is meant to be simple and informational.
  • It does not exist to build a profile on visitors.
  • If that changes materially, this page should change too.
